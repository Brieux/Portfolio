Dungeons & Dragons

During my first year of a Computer Science degree, we had to create a turn-based RPG combat system. I decided to build on the concept of Dungeons & Dragons, creating an entire adventure through a monster-filled dungeon in a text-based format.
The player can choose the number of heroes in their team from a selection of 17 different heroes.
Additionally, the game can be played PvP with a friend or allow the computer to play autonomously using an AI system.
