INSERM - Voyage au coeur des vaisseaux sanguins (NDA)

During a year I've worked with INSERM's scientific and doctors to create a serious game about blood donation. The aim of the game was to teach people the utility of giving their blood and all the mechanical process of each blood cells.

We've decided together to use the VR Headset to get a better immersive experience and to use the easy controls from VR Games
The game is light in term of gameplay because we didn't want to block the player against the game and getting frustrated to not reach any informations.

Through the whole game scientific facts are displayed using books which can be fund on the ground.
One of the most key of the game was to create something correct in terms of mechanical process & facts. The game is not realease on stores yet but it is use in blood giving's truck in Grand Est area and only there.
